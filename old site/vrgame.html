Information

For my recent school assignment, I created my first VR game using the Oculus Quest. The game is set on a tranquil island where piles of sand randomly emerge. When players dig up these sand piles, they discover hidden treasures, earning points.

The beauty of this game is its infinite replayability, ensuring players can keep exploring and accumulating points. I tackled the project independently and had the privilege of presenting it to the class. This experience has not only fulfilled the assignment requirements but has also ignited my passion for VR game development.
