DiscoJam

English

for this school assignment we a game that looks and feels good. And because we all five enjoy rhythm games we decided to choose this. We named our game Disco Jam, why the name Disco jam? Disco Jam is based on 80s disco, Jamming can be dancing but can also be meant as fighting.

The game will be like Streetfighter and guitar hero.

There are arrows that move upwards, and you have to hit them in the circle to get points and when the arrow is perfect in the circle you get 2 points. With the points you can activate an attack on the opponent. The player loses hp if the other person gets hit by an attack The Players play this until one dies or if the song is finished. Highest HP wins! If the hp is the same, then the player with the highest score wins.
